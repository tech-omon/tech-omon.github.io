About Us

OnMyOwnNow is a shop based on a collection of independent individual experiences and for the authentic YOU. The idea is not to lean but design your own life and be your authentic self, independent of any judgement or limiting beliefs. It is never too late to start at any point, you can always be on your own NOW.

OMON offers a multi-sensory studio space connecting to ‘The Self’ and moves beyond 6the traditional gallery space, borders, judgment, limiting beliefs offering a range of merchandise that is authentic, therapeutic, emotional and accessible to everyone, whether it is as significant as a print, homeware, clothing or a postcard. We make limited editions (since there is only one YOU) serving as an extension of our artistic practices.

Each piece is intended to lead you to positivity, self-discovery and emotional growth through a blend of humorous, enlightening and revealing products.

OMON is dedicated to YOU. May you realize the strength within you.

We invite you to the journey of imagination and creative direction.

With you in the flow,
